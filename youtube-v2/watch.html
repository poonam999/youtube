Defending champions France stormed into the quarter-finals in #Qatar2022 with a dominant display against Poland. Kylian Mbappe starred for Didier Deschamps' side to start off with an assist & a brilliant second-half brace.

Despite missing an open goal at the back post, Olivier Giroud put France in front in the 44th minute. With this goal, he became the record-goalscorer for Les Bleus. Twin strikes by Mbappe in the second half put the result beyond any doubt.

Keeping up with the spirit of being the #WorldsGreatestShow, late drama ensured that Poland were awarded a penalty which Robert Lewandowski converted with ease to bag a consolation.
